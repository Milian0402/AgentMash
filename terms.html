Terms

Use

Use the app with content you have permission to review. Do not rely on the score as legal, financial, medical, or safety advice.

Ownership

Your review data and uploaded artifacts stay under your control. The app does not claim ownership of content you add.

Generated feedback

Feedback packets are aids for creative and product judgement. Review them before using them in automated workflows, training sets, or customer-facing decisions.

The app is provided as-is. Review all outputs yourself before publishing or using them commercially.
