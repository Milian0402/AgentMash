Privacy

AgentMash is designed for private, local-first judgement of AI-generated work.

Data collection

The app does not collect, sell, share, or transmit personal data. Reviews, notes, uploads, and profile settings stay in browser storage on the device that runs the app.

User content

Images and text added to the deck are stored locally unless the user exports a JSON profile file. Exported files are controlled by the user.

Network use

The app does not call analytics, ads, AI APIs, or third-party services. The service worker only caches local app files for offline use.

Agent feedback packets

Feedback packets are generated in the browser from local review data. In this local-first build they are copied or downloaded by the user, not transmitted by the app.

Contact

Support details live on the support page. A real public support inbox still needs to be chosen before app store submission or a paid launch.
